#Compendium of stats that can be modified by adding/multiplication
#lg_attack - Damage done by light guns "Light guns are better at firing at smaller ships"
#lg_armor_piercing - Armor pierceing on light guns
#hg_attack - Damage done by the heavy guns. "heavy guns are better at firing at larger shpis"
#hg_armor_piercing - HG armor pierceing
#anti_air_attack - AA stats
#torpedo_attack - Torpedo attack buffs
#sub_attack - Effectiveness against subs
#mines_planting - Mine planting abilities
#mines_sweeping - sweeping abilities
#surface_visibility - Represents a ship's profile. The higher the value the more visible a ship becomes
#sub_visibility - Same as surface but for submarines
#surface_detection - Ability to detect surface vessels
#sub_detection - Same as above but for submarines
#armor_value - Armor is a value that repsents a shpi's steel decking thickness. It protects ship from attack
#max_strength -
#carrier_size - Deck size. Just has to do with planes on carrier
#naval_speed - Obviously dont need to explain
#naval_range - Range
#fuel_consumption 0- Consupmption of fuel
#build_cost_ic - build cost
#reliability - overall reliability of the ship
